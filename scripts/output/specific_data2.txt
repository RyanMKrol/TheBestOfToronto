    <h1>The Best Irish Pubs in Toronto</h1>

    Posted by <a href="/author/benjohnson">Ben Johnson</a>
<span class="date"> / June  3, 2013
</span>
    </p>

    <div class="content">
        <p><img src="http://www.blogto.com/upload/2013/06/201363-irish-pubs-toronto.jpg" width="590" height="389" alt="Irish Pubs Toronto"/>The best Irish pubs in Toronto offer more than a pint of a Guinness and a handful of vaguely traditional menu options. Sure, some of them are about as <em>Oirish</em> as Tom Cruise's accent in the movie <a href="http://youtu.be/wAebotpLVOI"><em>Far and Away</em></a> and have as much to do with a traditional Emerald Isle experience as the Lucky Charms Leprechaun, but what they do all have going for them is a healthy does of that famous Irish hospitality.</p>

<p>They're places with a neighbourly vibe and welcoming atmosphere where you might feel just as at home drowning your sorrows as you would kicking up a jig; and, of course, fresh kegs of the black stuff don't hurt either.</p>
